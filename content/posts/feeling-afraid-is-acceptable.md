<blockquote>Feeing afraid is acceptable,

but letting it affect your actions is not.

- L. E. Modesitt Jr., The Towers of the Sunset, pg 330</blockquote>

<p>Train yourself to recognize your fears and set them aside.</p>


&nbsp;

This is just a re-statement of the <a href="http://fear.isthemindkiller.com/2021/03/08/litany-against-fear/">Litany Against Fear</a>. After all, you will feel fear - but you must recognize it, and repeat the Litany until the fear has gone past.
