<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>I must not fear.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>Fear is the mind-killer.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>Fear is the little-death that brings total obliteration.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>I will face my fear.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>I will permit it to pass over me and through me.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>And when it has gone past I will turn the inner eye to see its path.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>Where the fear has gone there will be nothing. </p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph --><p>Only I will remain.</p><!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>- <strong>Frank Herbert, </strong><a href="https://smile.amazon.com/Dune-Chronicles-Book-1/dp/0441013597/">Dune</a></p>
