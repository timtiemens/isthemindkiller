<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When comparing stupidity to ignorance, ignorance is by far to be preferred.  Ignorance has an easy cure - exposure.  Once you've read and understood <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wronger_than_wrong">"More Wrong Than Wrong</a>" and "Just winning an argument does not make you right", your world has expanded for the better.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Unless, of course, you are inflicted with the stupidity disease.  If you continue to argue that "Evolution has flaws and so does intelligent design, so they are both equal." or "You must wear a mask or you will kill Nana.", you have been inflicted with stupidity.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You may be winning arguments by using the "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Appeal_to_emotion">Appeal to Emotion</a>" fallacy (or by using other <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Informal_fallacy">informal fallacies</a>), but that doesn't make you right.</p>
